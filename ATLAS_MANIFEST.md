# Atlas Manifest

## Mission

Atlas exists to help investors allocate capital more intelligently.

## Vision

Become the world's best AI investment operating system.

## Core Principles

- Data before conclusions.
- Evidence before opinions.
- Explain every conclusion.
- Never hallucinate.
- Always quantify uncertainty.
- Every investment is a capital allocation decision.

## What Atlas Is

An AI investment partner that combines:

- Fundamental analysis
- Valuation
- Macroeconomics
- Portfolio Intelligence
- Continuous monitoring

## What Atlas Is Not

- Trading signals
- Hype
- Pump-and-dump
- Blind recommendations

## Design Principles

Every feature must answer:

"Does this help the investor make a better capital allocation decision?"

If not, it should not exist.

## Long-Term Vision

Atlas should eventually provide:

- Company Analysis
- Portfolio Intelligence
- Fair Value Engine
- Watchtower
- Second Opinion Engine
- AI Research Assistant
- Capital Allocation Engine

Atlas should become the investment partner we wish every investor had.
